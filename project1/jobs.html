JD101 - Junior Software Developer

Salary Range

$65,000 - $85,000 AUD per year + superannuation

Overview

We're looking for a passionate and driven Junior Software Developer to join our growing development team. You'll be working closely with senior developers and project managers to build, test, and maintain high-quality software solutions. This is a great opportunity to grow your skills in a supportive, fast-paced environment that values learning and innovation.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Collaborate with the team to develop new features and maintain existing applications.
  2. Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.
  3. Participate in code reviews and incorporate feedback to improve code quality.
  4. Assist in testing, debugging, and deployment processes.
  5. Work closely with UX/UI designers to implement user-friendly interfaces.
  6. Learn and adapt to new technologies and frameworks as needed.

Required Skills & Experience

  • Basic knowledge of one or more programming languages (e.g., Python, C#, JavaScript).
  • Understanding of software development principles and version control systems (preferably Git).
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn.
  • Ability to work both independently and in a team setting.
  • Good communication skills and attention to detail.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with web development frameworks (e.g., React, Django, ASP.NET).
  • Experience working on personal or academic software projects.
  • Exposure to databases (e.g., SQL, MongoDB) and RESTful APIs.

What You'll Get

  • Mentorship from experienced developers.
  • Access to training materials and courses.
  • Opportunity to work on real-world projects from day one.
  • A relaxed and flexible working environment with a focus on growth.

CS205 - Cybersecurity Specialist

Salary Range

$95,000 - $120,000 AUD per year + superannuation

Overview

We're looking for an experienced and proactive Cybersecurity Specialist to strengthen our security posture and lead key initiatives across infrastructure, application, and cloud environments. You'll play a central role in detecting and responding to threats, securing our systems, and driving continuous improvement across security processes and controls.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Monitor, investigate, and respond to security incidents across networks, endpoints, and cloud environments.
  2. Perform vulnerability assessments and coordinate remediation efforts with relevant teams.
  3. Implement, maintain, and tune security tools (SIEM, IDS/IPS, EDR, firewalls, etc.).
  4. Develop and enforce security policies, procedures, and technical standards.
  5. Conduct threat hunting activities and proactively identify emerging risks.
  6. Work with DevOps and IT teams to secure CI/CD pipelines, APIs, and infrastructure-as-code environments.
  7. Assist in internal and external audits, risk assessments, and compliance reviews (e.g., ISO 27001, SOC 2).

Required Skills & Experience

  • 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity, IT security operations, or related technical fields.
  • Solid understanding of network protocols, operating systems (Linux & Windows), and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
  • Hands-on experience with security tools such as Splunk, CrowdStrike, Tenable, Palo Alto, or similar.
  • Strong incident response and forensics capabilities.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills, especially under pressure.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Relevant certifications such as CISSP, CISM, OSCP, CEH, or equivalent.
  • Familiarity with secure coding practices and working with development teams.
  • Experience with scripting or automation tools (Python, Bash, PowerShell).
  • Exposure to regulatory frameworks like NIST CSF, ISO 27001, or GDPR.

What You'll Get

  • Ownership of impactful security initiatives.
  • Continuous professional development with training and certification support.
  • Flexible work arrangements and a collaborative team environment.
  • A security-first culture where your ideas and expertise are valued.
